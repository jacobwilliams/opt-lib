private subroutine muller(me, ax, bx, fax, fbx, xzero, fzero, iflag)

Improved Muller method (for real roots only). Will fall back to bisection if any step fails.

Reference

  • D. E. Muller, "A Method for Solving Algebraic Equations Using an Automatic Computer", Mathematical Tables and Other Aids to Computation, 10 (1956), 208-215.
  • Regular Muller here (Julia version): https://github.com/JuliaMath/Roots.jl/blob/97dbe2e178656e39b7f646cff278e4e985d60116/src/simple.jl
